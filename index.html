  • Topics
  • Contact

De-Code

A combination of the two most popular coding events Revengg and Debugging, De-Code challenges all minds to exercise their grey matter. Go back to the good old days of C, C++ and Java to solve the problems at hand!

  • Rules
  • Format
  • Contact

  • Participate individually or maximum in a team of 2.

  • The event is open to all students from all disciplines.

  • The event will consist of 2 Rounds.

  • Use of Internet is not allowed.

  • Round 1 – The elimination round having 45 mins to solve basic debugging and coding questions.

  • Final Round – 1 hour of reverse coding. The most efficient code will win De-Code.

  • The decision of our judges will be final and binding to all.

Online Photography

Exhibit your extraordinary photography skills.
PS: You don't need a DSLR to become a photographer.

  • Rules
  • Contact

  • The participant has to like the Übertech 2015 page only.

  • The participant has to like the Übertech 2013 Facebook page (this page) in order to enter the competition.

  • The participant can upload pictures for all the categories but can upload only one picture per category.

  • Multiple entries will lead to disqualification.

  • The participant must be the real owner of the picture and plagiarism will not be entertained.

  • No watermarks or any other marking must be present on the picture.

  • The participant must give picture information (like ISO, shutter speed, aperture, focal length, etc).

  • The participant must provide a caption and the personal information (Name, College Name, Current Year).

  • Top 10 pictures with the highest number of likes will be judged by our judges and the picture will be evaluated with 75% of (total number of likes) and 25% of (critic interest).
